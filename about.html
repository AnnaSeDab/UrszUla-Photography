- About UrszUla -

My name is Urszula and I am a professional photographer based in Maidstone - Kent. I have over 10 years experience and throughout the years I've done hundreds of photo sessions. I specialise in portraits and newborn photos. I use my experience, technical expertise, creativity and composition skills to produce high quality images. Every picture tells the story and I always focus on human emotions which makes the pictures look natural. When taking pictures of newborns I am very patient and understanding of the needs of the baby and the parents trying my best to make this experience as stress free as possible to all involved.
