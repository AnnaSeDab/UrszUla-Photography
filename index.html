-Welcome!-

My name is Urszula and I have been a photographer for over 10 years.
If you want to book a portrait or baby photoshoot either for yourself or as a present for someone else, you are in the right place.
I am also specialising in restoration of old photos.
Have a look around, browse the photos, read about my experience and do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or would like to book a photo shoot.
